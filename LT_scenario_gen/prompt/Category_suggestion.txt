The basic rule for determining whether a category can be recommended is:

- A: Empty rate > 50%
- B: 20% < Empty rate < 50%
- C: Empty rate < 20%

- A: 1~2 vehicles
- B: 3~5 vehicles
- C: 6~7 vehicles
- D: 8+ vehicles
